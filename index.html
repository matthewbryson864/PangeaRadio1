Eli Spiral

Episode 8 | Progressive House

Next up for Pangea Radio, we have a very special guest - Eli Spiral. Eli is one of the founding members of Pangea Productions in its original conception over 20 years ago (under the name Labyrinth). Now based in Boulder Colorado, his passion for deep, progressive, and psychedelic music has persisted and he has now released music on a number of top-notch labels including Droid9, JourneyDeep Records, JOOF Recordings, Balkan Connection, Forescape Digital, Lost Language, and many more. His productions feature thick atmospheres and melodies and combine a vast amount of influences. His latest dj set for Pangea Radio is no different and showcases his deep understanding of progressive house & trance music.

Chasing

Episode 7 | Deep House

This week for Pangea Radio is a very talented artist based in Montgomery Alabama - Chasing. Chasing (Jake Pugh) has been helping craft the nightlife scene in Montgomery for over half a decade now with his event production Gravity. He is a resident for ProjectB. in Atlanta and has opened for many influential artists including Yokoo, Jon Charnis, Sepp, and Elke Kleijn to name a few. His sets are very hypnotic and combine elements of deep house, afrohouse, and minimal with lots of atmospheres and warm textures. We are very happy to have him join us for Pangea 15 this year and we hope you enjoy this mix!

Raffidelic

Episode 4 | Psychedelic Trance

We are very honored to present another high-quality mix from an insanely talented DJ - Raffidelic. Bringing Pangea back to its roots of Psychedelic Trance, this mix is an absolute ride through deep basslines, futuristic melodies, and spacey atmospheres - all mixed perfectly. Now based in Raleigh NC, Raffi has been involved in the psytrance scene for many years in Brooklyn and throughout the US with his ‘Beat Samsara’ productions, helping bring together the legendary Dreamtime Festival. This mix showcases his nighttime sounds with chunky basslines and thick psychedelic soundscapes. He has upcoming shows in NY, Maryland, and Mexico and we are also very excited to have him join us for his first Welcome to Pangea in June. We hope you enjoy this high-octane ride deep into space.
